This program visualizes epipolar geometry between two images.

The user needs to:

- Load two images (a stereo pair);
- Click on one of the windows when the keypoints and matches have been computed;
- Click on one of the windows when the inliers have appeared;
- Click on a pixel in one of the images to display the corresponding epipolar line in the other image;
- Right click and then left click to close the program.
